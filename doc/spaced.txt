INTRODUCTION                                    *spaced*

Plugin for dealing with spacing and special character issues.

                                                *cT*
cT                      Toggle spaces/tabs at beginning of current line

                                                *c;*
c;                      Remove unnecessary characters at end of current line (spaces and windows line-endings)
